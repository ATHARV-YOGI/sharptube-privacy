Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 11, 2025

SharpTube is a Chrome extension designed to help users stay focused by removing distractions from the YouTube interface.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. SharpTube runs entirely in your browser and does not send or receive any data from external servers.

Permissions Used

  • storage: to save user preferences locally, such as focus mode toggle state.
  • content scripts: applied only to YouTube pages to hide distracting elements.

These permissions are used solely for core functionality and never for tracking or analytics.

Third Parties

SharpTube does not use third-party services or analytics. No information is ever shared or sold.
